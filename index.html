About DataCraft

DataCraft is a browser-based synthetic data generation platform designed for developers, analysts, QA engineers, educators, and businesses.

In modern software development, testing with production data is a massive security and privacy risk. DataCraft solves this by allowing you to instantly spin up thousands of rows of realistic, localized data without ever making a server request.

All datasets are generated locally within your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

DataCraft operates on a strict zero-data-retention model. We do not track users.

2. Local Processing

  • No server processing occurs when generating your data.
  • No cookies are used for tracking.
  • Everything generated stays on your local machine.

3. GDPR Compliance

Because no personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on our servers, DataCraft is inherently privacy-friendly and fully compliant with GDPR regulations.

FAQ

How does DataCraft work?

It uses browser-side JavaScript to randomly compile and format synthetic data based on the schemas you select.

Is my data private?

Yes. 100% of the generation happens in your RAM. Nothing is sent to a server.

Can I use generated data commercially?

Absolutely. The generated data is free of copyright and completely yours to use.
